# **App Name**: Double Black Supply

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Product Grid: Display product categories (Urban Equipment, Performance Trail, Limited Editions) in a visually striking Bento Grid layout, consistent with the brand's brutalist aesthetic.
- Product Detail Pages: Offer comprehensive pages for each product, featuring large-scale imagery, detailed descriptions, technical specifications (impermeability, breathability, thermal resistance), and purchasing options.
- Shopping Cart & Secure Checkout: Enable users to add products to a shopping cart, review selected items, and proceed through a secure, high-performance checkout process.
- Hero Section Immersion: Implement a high-impact hero section with a slow-motion video showcasing extreme challenges, dynamic headlines, and subheadlines to immediately establish the brand's identity.
- Elite Squad Newsletter Signup: Integrate a clear and minimalist form in the footer, titled 'ENTRE PARA O ESQUADRÃO DE ELITE,' for users to subscribe to exclusive updates and limited releases.
- AI-Powered Copy Tool: A generative AI tool to assist in crafting or refining product descriptions and marketing copy, ensuring the challenging, direct, premium, and inspiring brand tone is consistently maintained.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background: True Black (#000000) for a brutalist, high-performance aesthetic, as explicitly requested by the user. This forms the canvas for high-contrast elements.
- Primary: Deep Horizon Blue (#4080BF), chosen to evoke high technology, deep resilience, and the vast, challenging horizons of extreme environments. It provides strong contrast and depth against the black background.
- Accent: Emergency Orange (#FF6A00), a vivid and commanding color (equivalent to 'Laranja Emergência' as requested by the user), strategically deployed for Calls-To-Action (CTAs), critical alerts, and other high-impact interactive elements to convey urgency and adrenaline.
- Secondary Contrast: Anthracite Gray (#2B313B), for subtle contrasts, text hierarchy, and secondary UI elements, offering a refined, industrial texture while maintaining a dark, sophisticated palette.
- Primary font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for all text. Its robust, techy, and slightly aggressive aesthetic perfectly aligns with the brutalist minimalism and 'caixa alta' requirement, suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Integrate the double black diamond symbol (◆◆) pervasively across the UI as separators, loading indicators, and minimalist functional icons, reinforcing the brand's exclusivity and technical focus. All icons should adhere to a sharp, brutalist, and robust style.
- Minimalist, brutalist layout characterized by generous use of full-bleed, high-granulation imagery featuring stormy mountains, wet asphalt, and macro shots of technical fabrics. Pages utilize high-impact sections, including a Bento Grid for product categories and distinct visual blocks for technical specifications.
- Subtle yet impactful high-performance animations for state changes and transitions, ensuring a crisp and responsive user experience. This includes smooth video playback in the hero section and snappy interactions without unnecessary fluff.
